01A — HOW TO BUY · 2-RAIL CHECKOUT

Every RENEJA9 build can be completed on one of two rails:

  • PAY WITH USD — Direct Stripe Checkout in dollars. Clean, simple payment for this exact PEN, CP, or Scroll.
  • PAY WITH RENE RINGS (R&R) — Use your $10 creative credits instead of paying in USD at checkout. One R&R equals $10 of covenant value on the inside rail.

If you don’t have R&R yet, first purchase credits once using the RENE RINGS (R&R) Credit product. After that, any PEN, Centerpiece, or PINS-on-PENS Scroll can be completed on the R&R rail without changing its authority, only the way it is funded.

USD defines the external invoice. R&R defines the internal creative ledger. The artifact, covenant, and registry entry stay the same.
